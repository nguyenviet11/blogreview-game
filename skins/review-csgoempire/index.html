10 CS2 Workshop Skins That Will Make You Say “Take My Money!”

- -
May 4, 2025
Skins
5 min read

The Counter-Strike Workshop is a place where true masterpieces are born, which will either end up in cases and explode the market, or forever remain in players’ dreams. We’ve once again gone through the freshest items and selected 10 skins that look so cool that VALVE simply must notice them. Ready to see what can add a little magic, humor, and swagger to the game? Then check out our selection and let’s dream together about how these beauties would look in your inventory!

MAC-10 | RADFALL

This MAC-10 looks like it just returned from a tour of Pripyat, and judging by its appearance, not empty-handed. The body features a bright radiation symbol, and the word “CAUTION” in Russian is written in screaming red paint. The greenish-yellow color palette with rusty streaks and worn areas adds a post-apocalyptic atmosphere to the skin, which is the main theme of the beloved Cache map. Unfortunately, the chances of adding this map are quite slim, but if it happens – we already have a decent candidate for inclusion in the Cache 2026 collection!

Zeus x27 | Shock Spell

This Zeus x27 design is like if you took a taser and sent it to study at Hogwarts. The purple body with bright blue lightning looks as if it was enchanted in a Defense Against the Dark Arts class. The main feature is the painted hand with a magic wand, from which magical energy bursts forth, ready to stun an enemy with a single touch. Add to this a slightly worn texture, and it seems like this Zeus has been through a couple of duels with Voldemort. Perfect for fans of the iconic saga and players who want to add a bit of magic to the game.

Sawed-Off | Hunter's Club

This shotgun looks like a genuine museum exhibit, but instead of a glass display case, it’s ready to appear in one of the cases or souvenir containers. The body is decorated with exquisite engravings depicting wild boars in the forest, as if this weapon belonged to some 19th-century aristocrat who traded hunting trips for CS matches. The golden-brown tones, carved patterns on the barrel and handle, as well as the wood texture make it look like an antique that accidentally made its way into a modern shooter. This design would be great to see on a more popular weapon, which would become a real hit among realism enthusiasts.

Glock-18 | Cyber Samurai

A popular cyberpunk vibe with an eastern accent. The black and red palette immediately catches the eye, and the handle features a detailed image of a samurai in armor who seems ready to enter battle right now. The characters “武士” (which translates to “samurai”) add authenticity, while small red accents like buttons and lines create the feeling that this weapon was assembled in the workshop of some Tokyo gunsmith from the future. This Glock is perfect for those who want their weapon to look like a scene from an anime, but at the same time not be too colorful and remain fairly restrained.

AK-47 | Bloom

The AK-47 “Sakura” is like if you took an assault rifle and sent it to a cherry blossom festival, but with a small adjustment: instead of tea and petals, you have a cool skin in your hands that will definitely appeal to most girls. Delicate pink and white tones cover the body, and floral patterns with cherry blossom branches create a feeling of spring lightness. The main star of the skin is the image of an anime girl with pink hair, who seems about to come alive right on the body. VALVE definitely needs to take note of this work and add a bit of freshness and tenderness to the game.

Keychain | Brick

This keychain in the form of a Nokia 3310 called “Brick” is pure old school and meme vibes in one package. The blue body with the inscription “BRICK” and a pixelated image on the screen refers to those times when phones were so durable that you could hammer nails with them. The buttons, antenna, and even the texture of the body look as if this keychain can really call your mom from the 2000s and play a midi ringtone instead of dial tones. Every old-timer who remembers what the first matches in CS 1.6 looked like will want to hang it on their weapon.

AWP | Cardboard

No money for a Dragon Lore? Make it in your craft class or in your dad’s garage! The body seems to be made of cardboard, and on it is drawn a dragon that looks like a schoolboy sketched it in just a couple of minutes! The inscription “DRAGON LORE FOR FREE” screams that this skin is pure trolling. Well, we already had an attempt with Doodle Lore, which was removed from the game a day after release, so why not make a similar attempt at a “cheap” copy of the legendary skin? Moreover, it really looks just like that. We’re sure this rifle would become a real hit, the main thing is to give it not too rare quality to guarantee a large number of copies.

Keychain | Little Boss

This golden keychain with a skull in a crown is a reference to the golden Crown (Foil) sticker. The inscription “BOSS” on the crown and massive chains on the chest immediately reveal the “daddy” on the server, which most players will gladly use. The gold trim with small details like the cross on the chain makes this accessory look like a trophy of some mafioso from the 90s. Even the texture looks as if the keychain was cast in pure gold – no scratches, just shine and chic. Hang it on your gun, and everyone around will immediately understand: you’re the boss here, and your AWP isn’t just a weapon, it’s the scepter of a king.

Desert Eagle | Kiss Of Death

Another weapon for those who want to kill with tenderness. The bright pink body with red and white swirls looks as if it was decorated for Valentine’s Day. The slide features painted hearts and a hand that seems to be shooting air kisses, and the fine lines add a romantic vibe. This skin is perfect for players who want to “lovingly” eliminate their opponents. Well, are you ready to break hearts on the map?

USP-S | Eastern Grace

This USP-S looks as if it was created for a tea ceremony. The white body with red and black accents is decorated with elegant patterns: on the silencer – floral ornaments that seem to have been drawn by a calligrapher with perfect handwriting. On the pistol itself, the silhouette of a girl with flowing hair attracts attention, as if from a Japanese engraving, and creates the feeling that this is a work of art, not a skin from Counter-Strike. Small details, like red buttons and edging, further emphasize the eastern vibe.
